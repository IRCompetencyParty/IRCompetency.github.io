The Party of Competence and Development

In Simple Terms, We Believe In:

A future for Iran built on Competency, not connections. Full Transparency in government. A strong, respected Iran that is a Partner with the World. And a deep commitment to Protecting Iran's Environment for our children.

Our vision is to build a prosperous, sovereign, and respected Iran where every citizen has the dignity of opportunity and the security of justice. We envision a nation defined by the competence of its people, the transparency of its government, peaceful engagement with the world, and harmony with its environment. We are unequivocally committed to the territorial integrity and national sovereignty of Iran.

Pillar 1: Good Governance

We will strive for a government that serves the people, not the powerful. We will work to dismantle the systems of corruption and cronyism that have held our nation back.

The Past: A System of Decay

  • ⛓️ Cronyism: Key roles based on connections, not skill.
  • 🔒 Lack of Transparency: Public funds and decisions hidden from view.
  • ⚖️ Unequal Justice: Laws applied differently based on status.
  • ⛪️ Religious Governance: State authority tied to religious doctrine.

Our Future: A System of Integrity

  • 🏛️ Secular Governance: Separation of religion and state.
  • 🎓 Merit-Based Service: An independent commission to ensure competence.
  • 🔍 Radical Transparency: A powerful anti-corruption agency.
  • ⚖️ Independent Judiciary: Rule of law applied equally to all.

Pillar 2: Foreign Policy

Isolation belongs in the past. Iran's strength lies in its ability to be a constructive and engaged partner on the world stage.

From Isolation to Partnership

Our foreign policy will be guided by the principle of "Friend to All, Partner for Peace." We will build a "Ring of Prosperity" with our neighbors and open our doors to tourism and investment, re-establishing Iran as a respected member of the international community.

  • 1Resolve disputes through diplomacy and de-escalation.
  • 2Launch a national initiative to become a world-class tourism destination.
  • 3Build a "Ring of Prosperity" with neighbors on joint economic projects.

Pillar 3: Democratic Safeguards

Power must be a trust, not a right. We will strive for a system that prevents the rise of dictatorship and protects the rights of every single Iranian.

Unity in Diversity

We support laws that explicitly protect the rights of all ethnic groups. We recognize Persian as the official language for national unity, while simultaneously promoting regional languages as a vital part of our national heritage.

Checks and Balances

We advocate for a parliamentary system with strict term limits. True national unity is built on trust and partnership, devolving power to local governments to strengthen our national bond.

Pillar 4: Environmental Sustainability

Our natural resources are the foundation of our national prosperity and well-being. We will act decisively to reverse the environmental crises that threaten our nation's very survival.

A Green Economic Shift

Unsustainable Path

Continuing on the current path means prioritizing water-intensive industries and agricultural practices that deplete our most precious resource.

  • 🏭 Subsidies for polluting industries like tile manufacturing.
  • 🍉 Cultivation of water-intensive crops like rice and watermelon.
  • 🚱 Neglect of our deteriorating water infrastructure.

Our plan is to declare a national water emergency, invest in sustainable technology, and shift our economy towards a green future.

  • 💧 Massive investment in desalination and water conservation.
  • 🏞️ Promoting high-value, low-impact sectors like eco-tourism and tech.
  • 🌲 Creating and expanding national parks to protect our natural heritage.
